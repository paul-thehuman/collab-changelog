Co.LLab Privacy Policy

AI-powered course authoring tool by The Human Co.

Last updated: 4 March 2026

● The short version

  • Co.LLab runs entirely on your computer — your data stays with you
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not include analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind
  • We do not create user accounts or require registration
  • We do not send any data to The Human Co. servers
  • Third-party APIs are optional and controlled by you

1. What Co.LLab is

Co.LLab is a desktop application for Windows and macOS that helps you create professional elearning courses using AI. It is built and maintained by The Human Co. (thehumanco.org).

The application runs locally on your computer. There are no Co.LLab servers, cloud services, or remote infrastructure involved in its operation.

2. Information we collect

None. Co.LLab does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device identifiers or machine IDs
  • Usage data, analytics, or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Crash reports
  • Browsing or activity history

There are no user accounts, no registration, and no login required to use Co.LLab.

3. Data stored on your computer

Co.LLab stores the following data locally on your machine and nowhere else:

Your API keys

If you choose to connect third-party services (Anthropic, Pexels, ElevenLabs), your API keys are saved in an encrypted local settings file on your computer. These keys are never transmitted to The Human Co. or any party other than the respective API provider.

Your course files

Course blueprints, generated content, images, and audio files are saved to a local folder on your computer (typically Documents/Co.LLab). This data never leaves your machine unless you choose to share it yourself.

Error logs

If something goes wrong, the app writes a local error log for debugging purposes. This log is never sent anywhere automatically.

4. Third-party services

Co.LLab can optionally connect to the following third-party APIs using API keys that you provide and control. Each service is entirely optional — the app works without them.

Service Purpose Data sent Required?
Anthropic (Claude) AI content generation Course titles, objectives, topics, and briefs Yes
Pexels Stock photography Image search queries (e.g. “leadership communication”) No
ElevenLabs Audio narration AI-generated voiceover scripts No
Important: No personal information, user names, learner data, or identifying details are sent to any third-party service. Only course content data (titles, topics, scripts) is transmitted, and only when you initiate a build.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Pexels Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs Privacy Policy

5. Analytics and tracking

Co.LLab does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking software. There are no cookies, no pixels, no fingerprinting, and no usage metrics of any kind.

6. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to share.

7. Children's privacy

Co.LLab is a professional course authoring tool intended for adult use. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

8. Data security

API keys stored by Co.LLab are encrypted on your local machine. All communication with third-party APIs occurs over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). Since no data is stored on our servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach from The Human Co.

9. Your rights

Since Co.LLab does not collect or store any personal data on our end, there is no data for us to access, correct, delete, or export on your behalf. All your data resides on your own computer and is fully under your control.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the application and removing the Co.LLab folder from your Documents directory.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Co.LLab after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
